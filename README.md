# 🤖 My AI Agent

A personal AI assistant platform inspired by OpenClaw.

## ✨ Features

- 📱 **Telegram Integration** - Chat with your AI from your phone
- 🧠 **Multiple AI Providers** - OpenAI (GPT-4) or Anthropic (Claude)
- 💾 **Memory System** - Remembers your conversations
- 🔒 **Private & Secure** - Runs on your own machine
